About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics, National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Li Qianxiao.

I graduated with a BSc in Pure and Applied Mathematics, Jilin University and an MSc in Mathematics, National University of Singapore.

My research areas include machine learning and dynamical systems, particularly focusing on applying neural networks to improve numerical algorithms and integrating Koopman operators with control systems for forward predictions and inverse problems.
